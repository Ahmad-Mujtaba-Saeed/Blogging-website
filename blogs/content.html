Types of Headache

1.Tension Headache:
2.Migraine Headache:
3.‌Cluster Headache:

1.Tension Headache:

In this headache, patient feel pain all over the head.It might be because of stress and over thinking .Stress may be because of work load, family pressure or job issues.It might be cured by doing some rest.





2.Migraine Headache:

In this type of headache, patient feel pain on one side of the brain.The patient suffered from nausea and also has the issue of light and sound intensity.It might last for days if not cured properly.





3.‌‌Cluster Headache: :

In this type of headache,the patient feel pressure around the eye.In this headache, eyes of the patient turns red.Its symptoms are tears in the eyes. It can last for months.

Reasons of Headache:



1.Stress:

Stress is the main factor which cause headache.Whenever you think about something repeatedly, it causes the tightens of your muscles.As a result, you feel your head heavy and stress leads to tension headache.



2.Lack of Sleep:

Go beyond, another reason is Lack of Sleep.Whenever, you are doing your job and don't take your proper sleep.It cause headache which can be cured by taking proper sleep.



3.Work Load:

Whenever you are doing multitask, you are increasing your workload.If you can't perform it on time, it leads to stress and stress leads to headache.

4.Hormones:

Go beyond, changes in hormones also lead to headache.Varying estrogen level can also cause headache especially in women. Because change in estrogen level can cause migraine.But this migraine affects women more as compared to men.

5.Caffeine Withdrawal:

Whenever, you are working at late hours, you often need coffee or tea. If you are used to take caffeine in coffee, it is harmful for health.Because it cause migraine and also trigger it. So, stop taking caffeine in your coffee because it leads to headache.
