What are the most played sports in the world

  • Tokyo Doe
  • 1 April 2022

The most popular sport in the world is a hotly debated topic. There are many different opinions on what the most popular sports might be and why they are so popular.

Here is the explanation of the most popular, must-watch sports in the world!



1. Football (Soccer) – Over 3.5 Billion Fans :



Football (Soccer) is one of the most popular sports in the world with over 3.5 billion fans around the globe. It is a physical and mental challenge that is enjoyed by people of all ages. The game is played on a field with a rectangular playing area divided into ninety-six equal squares. There are eleven players on each side and the object of the game is to score by moving the ball into the opponent's goal. Football Soccer is a fast-paced, exciting game that is often compared to basketball because of its similarities in moving the ball around the court and scoring points.

Why soccer is popular?

Because it is a social sport. It can keep many fans on the edge of their seat because of its unpredictable consequences. This results to exciting and tense games that keep fans on the edge of their seats. In addition, it's a social game because spectators can also gather together in the stadiums and bars to cheer for their favorite teams.



2. Cricket – Over 2.5 Billion Fans :



Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It is considered the national sport of England and the Commonwealth. The game is played on a rectangular field with a running track around it, and is divided into two halves of thirty-two overs each. The objective of the game is to score as many runs as possible. The game is played on a hard surface that is usually grass.

The game is an old one, dating back to the late seventeenth century. It was first played in England on a field near the present day Lord's cricket ground in London. The game gradually spread to other parts of the world

Why cricket is popular?

There are many reasons why cricket has become so popular. One of them is that there is a lot of action. The other is that it is a very strategic game. We are used to making quick decisions on the fly. Cricket also has a strong social aspect that makes it so popular. This sport is often played as part of a team or community, bringing people together from all walks of life.



3. Hockey – Over 2 Billion Fans :



Hockey is one of the most popular sports in the world. It is the national game of Pakistan. It is a fast-paced, physical game that is enjoyed by people of all ages. Hockey is a popular winter sport in Canada, the United States, Europe, and Asia. There are many different types of hockey, including indoor hockey, ice hockey, street hockey, and roller hockey. Hockey is a great sport to enjoy with friends.

Why hockey is popular?


This fast-paced, action-packed sport requires a lot of skill and precision but can be fun for both players and spectators.

The game of Ice Hockey is a world-renowned sport. If you are one of those who do not know much about it, there is no better time than now to start watching hockey matches.



4. Tennis – Over 1 Billion Fans:



Tennis is one of the oldest and most popular sports in the world. It is a very physical sport that requires a lot of stamina and agility. The object of the game is to hit a ball over a net and into the other team’s court. The players use a variety of strokes to hit the ball, including backhanded, forehand, and serve. Tennis is a great sport to get your exercise and can be enjoyed by people of all ages.

Why tennis is popular?


Tennis is a favourite sport of people of all ages and socioeconomic backgrounds because of its fast-paced action and high-stakes competition. Tennis is a beloved sport for many reasons, whether you play competitively or just enjoy watching from the sidelines.



5. Volleyball – Over 900 Million Fans :



Volleyball is a sport that can be enjoyed by all. It is a great workout that can be done indoors or outdoors. It is a fun sport that can be played with friends or family. It is a great way to stay active and healthy.

It's critical to comprehend what volleyball is in its truest form. This sport essentially consists of two teams competing against one another on a court that is divided in half by a net. The goal of the game is to hit the ball over the net into your opponent's half of the court using your hands or arms.

Why volleyball is popular?

This exciting sport creates a dynamic and thrilling game that appeals to players and spectators alike. Whether you are a casual fan or an avid player, here is everything you need to know about volleyball.


Conclusion

There are many different sports that are played around the world. However, the most popular sports are basketball, football, and soccer. These sports are played by millions of people all over the world.
