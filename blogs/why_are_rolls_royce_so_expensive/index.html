Why are rolls royce so expensive

  • Tokyo Doe
  • 23 April 2022
  • 3
  • 302
Tweet Pin Email

The word Rolls Royce automatically evokes thoughts of luxury. One of the most comfortable and opulent auto manufacturers in the world is Rolls Royce. The cost of any other car you have ever owned is much higher than the cost of this incredibly comfortable experience. These vehicles are essentially made to order. As a result, extra care is taken to ensure customer satisfaction while manufacturing each vehicle.


Why so expensive?

(Top 5 reasons)

1.Hand-Made Car Detailing

2.Color selection

3.The Paint Job

4.Starlight Headliner

5.Brand


1.Hand-Made Car Detailing





Coach stripe painters and car detailers are employed directly by Rolls-Royce. There is one man in the entire business whose sole responsibility it is to paint coach stripes on cars, if you ever decide you want one. You should be prepared to wait a while because they only have one painter working on the car detailing and pinstriping. The cost of detailing your car will increase with the length of time it takes. This is a result of the painter's need to travel to where your car is.

It might not be where he's currently employed. This also applies if you decide to have your car painted with coach stripes after it has been delivered to your house. They'll send one of their painters to visit you.




2.Color selection




The colour is one of the most expensive factors that affects a Rolls-price. Royce's The first question a representative will ask you when you meet them is what colour you want the car to be. They've had success matching cars to a particular lipstick shade or your favourite dog's coat colour. According to Rolls-Royce, there are more than 44,000 different colour options.

They elevate the act of painting your car's exterior to the status of a luxury. They will reproduce the shade if they don't have it. This contributes to the high cost of the paint job. They will use a variety of paint colours until they achieve the exact shade you desire. That even includes colours that aren't offered elsewhere.





3.The Paint Job




The colour of Rolls-Royce vehicles is never referred to as a paint job. It goes far beyond that. Your car is given a name after you after you select a colour for it. Rolls-Royce will get in touch with you if anyone else later wants to use that colour. They will request your consent before using the colour of the other person's car. Saying no is never a problem.

They want to make sure that everyone has the opportunity to have a special colour that is entirely their own, so they are asking you. Painting the car is referred to as a Surface Finish. Your car is not taken to the paint shop. They transport it to the Surface Finish Center instead. The automobile will get at least seven layers of paint here. Thats why it is more expensive


.


4.Starlight Headliner




The Starlight Headliner is a standard feature in every Rolls-Royce automobile. There are numerous holes in the top of the car, each with lights inside. Your car's ceiling will resemble the night sky as a result. Many owners adore this lovely feature. You can always decide to remove the Starlight Headliner if you don't like it. However, because it is a standard Rolls-Royce feature, many people decide to leave the Starlight Headliner in place. Any observer will recognise it for what it is. Making the Starlight Headliner requires 16 hours.

Rolls-Royce automobiles can be pricey because of this. Every Starlight Headliner is made by hand. They will cut holes in the headliner's fabric and then run a fibre optic light through them.
Customers can also choose to implement their own starry designs or favorite constellations.



5.Brand




You anticipate paying a high price when you purchase a Rolls-Royce vehicle. That's because the brand is everything. BMW has distinguished itself in the Rolls-Royce market by offering the pinnacle of luxury vehicles. Everyone is aware that luxury has a high cost. The company also advertises its ability to customise. The business will comply with your requests regardless of what you need or how long they take. Simply anticipate a high bill when it's finished.

Rolls-Royce is costly because it has a reputation for being so. It reinforces itself. A Rolls-Royce automobile isn't known for its performance or speed. It is renowned for its opulent attributes and capacity to serve as a status symbol. It costs a lot because it's well-made.


Conclusion

Rolls Royce automobiles are not mass produced in the same way that other vehicles are. The construction of a Rolls Royce car would not begin until the buyer placed an order.
