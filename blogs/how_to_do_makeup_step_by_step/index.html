10 steps to do makeup easily



1.Moisturizer:




First of all ,you should apply moisturizer on your face .A moisturizer is a cream that you put on your skin to make it feel softer and smoother.Moisturizer is used to prevent skin from dryness.



This step is necessary for you if you have a dry skin


2.Use Primer :




Second step is to apply primer on your face.Primer is a step between your skincare routine and makeup application that creates a smooth surface on your face and keeps makeup from caking around blemishes or wrinkles.

Makeup artists have used primer to help their makeup last longer.Begin by applying a light layer of primer to the centre of your face, dabbing it in with your fingers or a makeup sponge. Allow a few minutes for the primer to dry before applying your foundation.


It helps your makeup to stay longer.


3.Apply Foundation:




Third step is to apply foundation on your face.You can used foundation all over your face to create an even base.It is also used to cover flaws and sometimes to change natural skin tone.Foundation can be in liquid,cream or in powder form.Choose right foundation shade that match your skin tone .



4.Apply Concealer:




Fourth step is to apply concealer. A concealer, also known as a colour corrector, is a type of cosmetic used to conceal blemishes. It is similar to foundation, but thicker, and is used to conceal different pigments by blending the flaw into the surrounding skin tone.It is generally applied under eyes ,around lips and nose.


5.Contouring:




Fifth step is to contour your face.Generally, contouring makeup is apply after foundation. Contouring can be in form of cream, liquid and stick that can be applied directly on top of foundation and then blend it .Contouring makeup can help to enhance your facial bone structure and create depth.




6.Apply Blush on :




Sixth step is to apply Blush .Apply blush on cheekbones below outer corner of eyes and blend well.Blush is generally used to give a skin flushed .It basically enhance your features.You can use blush in every day makeup look .





7.Use Highlighter:




Next step is to apply highlighter . Highlighter is generally used on cheeks ,Nose,jawlines and forehead .When we use highlighter on our face it looks more attractive . That's why ,Highlighter is important in makeup .





8.Apply Eyeshadows:




Eyeshadows are applied on eyelids to draw attention to the wearer's eyes or appear more attractive.We can apply eyeshadows on our eyes according to our outfit.
Eyeshadows basically enhance our personality and it helps us to look more beautiful and attractive.





9.Apply Eyeliner:




This step in makeup is more important.You can also use this step in everyday makeup look.Using an eyeliner,you can make your eyes appear more rounded,larger or sharper depends on your mood . .





10.Apply Lipstick:




The Last and final step in makeup is to apply Lipstick. Lipstick gives makeup a final look .You look more confident and elegant. Lipstick defines your lips and brightens your smile.


Conclusion

You should know these step before doing makeup, 1.Moisturizer ... 2.Use Primer ... 3.Apply Foundation ...
